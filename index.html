About Me

Hi! I'm Carles, a mechanical engineering student with a passion for innovation, efficiency, and solving real-world problems. My background blends hands-on engineering with a strategic, business-oriented mindset—shaped through experiences in robotics, product design, and financial analysis.

I've worked on a wide range of projects, from building RC robots and designing functional prototypes to conducting investment analysis and improving manufacturing processes. I enjoy using tools like CAD and Excel not just to design things, but to make informed, impactful decisions.

Professionally, I've interned at Amazon in both Kansas City and Miami, where I contributed to operational logistics and process optimization within large-scale fulfillment environments. I focused on identifying inefficiencies, analyzing performance data, and supporting initiatives that enhanced overall flow and productivity. I also interned at Talgo, the high-speed train manufacturer, where I worked in maintenance and quality control, gaining hands-on experience with complex mechanical systems and large-scale industrial operations.

Outside of work, I'm always looking for new challenges—whether that's launching a side project, diving into a new technology, or exploring the outdoors with friends. I believe the best solutions happen where engineering, creativity, and business intersect.
